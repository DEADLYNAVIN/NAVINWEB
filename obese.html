Obese Diet Plan

Hi your BMI is 30 or above, you're considered obese. Here's a diet plan to help you achieve a healthy weight:

  • Breakfast: Scrambled egg whites with spinach and avocado
  • Mid-morning snack: A small handful of walnuts and green tea
  • Lunch: Grilled turkey with steamed broccoli and brown rice
  • Afternoon snack: Sliced cucumber and hummus
  • Dinner: Grilled fish with steamed asparagus and quinoa
  • Before bed: A small bowl of Greek yogurt with chia seeds
